Dr. Alistair Vance

Dr. Vance brings over 20 years of experience at the intersection of international political economy, strategic intelligence, and cross-cultural advisory. Former senior fellow at the Centre for Geopolitics, his work has shaped policy frameworks for G20 institutions and multinational boards. He is the author of 4 reference books and 30+ peer-reviewed articles, with a focus on systemic risk and economic statecraft.

Frequent keynote speaker at WTO, World Economic Forum, and leading business schools. Fluent in 5 languages, Dr. Vance leads Stratara’s research agenda with a commitment to actionable, data-driven foresight.
